June 2018

Dream Bike

My vision for my ultimate electric bike has evolved over the years. I think the application I've thought about most has been a lightweight, fast commuter ebike that is nimble enough to go anywhere I need to go in a day while still maintaining enough performance to keep up with cars when roads or schedule dictate.

The weight requirement pretty much eliminates any currently available hub motor, although if the ultimate lightweight wheel motor ever gets built, it could probably get close to providing the desired power in a light enough package.

Inspirations:

  • recumpence lightweight full suspension left hand drive
  • paul daniel's custom crank drive
  • two56's two stage bottom bracket jackshaft left hand drive

Specifications:

  • lightweight (under 60lbs with batteries)
  • 45 mph cruising speed
  • left hand chain for motor
  • quiet motor

Future features:

  • IGH rear hub
  • belt pedal drive
  • torque sensing bottom bracket
